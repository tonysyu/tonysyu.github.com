Figure: Fog tunnel

Fog tunnel for imaging the flow and deposition of water droplets onto substrates. We use an ultrasonic nebulizer (which basically just vibrates a small container of water really quickly) to generate small droplets. This "fog" is carried by an air flow over a substrate, and we use a microscope objective to capture images.
