Figure: Shapes of hanging flag

Images of a long, thin sheet, i.e. "flag" (black) clamped to a jet-nozzle (green in image). Water, which flows along one side of the flag, induces bending. The flow velocity is steadily increased from left to right. An analytical model (blue line) matches experiments well at high flow rates.
